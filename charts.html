📊 Financial Insights Dashboard

🔵 Bar Chart – Debt-to-Equity vs Funding
Visualizes the company's debt-to-equity ratio against funding sources across fiscal years. Higher bars represent greater leverage or funding amounts.
📉 Line Chart – Expense vs Funding Trend
Shows the monthly expenses and funding inflows. Tracks the alignment of costs with funding over time, providing insights for budget planning.
📊 Combo Chart – Operating Costs vs Funding
Combines bar and line charts to represent operating costs and funding sources, offering a clear view of financial balance on a monthly basis.
🍰 Pie Chart – Financial Health by Company
Displays a pie chart that highlights the financial health of each company. Larger segments indicate more significant contributions or stability.
📊 Horizontal Bar Chart – Expense Category Trends
Organizes expenses by their trend status—whether they are declining, stable, or growing. Essential for cost control and strategic decisions.
💥 Bubble Chart – Revenue Growth
Displays company revenue growth using bubble sizes. Larger bubbles represent higher growth or market presence, offering a visual scale of performance.
